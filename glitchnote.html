What is GlitchNote

GlitchNote is a startup company that develops mobile games founded by me and some fellow university students.


ByteJam

Currently we are developing a game called ByteJam. This game is inspired by the popular mobile game Block Blast but with the addition of musical elements that change as you play.

The game works like Block Blast but when you clear lines, specific music tracks will play, such as melody, chords, bass, or drums. All of the tracks are at the same tempo and key so it always goes together well. There are hundreds of different combinations as there are over 35 different tracks for just the first level.
